Brief Introduction

My name is Wenqing Qian (钱文清). I am now a second-year doctoral student in the Michigan Program of Survey and Data Science (MPSDS) at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am also a graduate student research assistant (GSRA) at the Health and Retirement Study (HRS), working in the Psychosocial and Aging Group led by Prof. Jacqui Smith. I am currently investigating some data quality issues of the Life History Mail Survey (LHMS), including item nonresponse and data consistency.

Previously, I was a guest researcher at the Department of Digital and Computational Demography in the Max Planck Institute for Demographic Research (MPIDR) between 2021-2023, mainly in collaboration with Dr. Ole Hexel (link to his personal website here!) and Prof. Emilio Zagheni.

I got my Master's degree in survey and data science from University of Michigan in 2024, and my Bachelor's degree in statistics from Fudan University in 2022.
